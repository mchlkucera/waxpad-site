Workflow inspiration

None of this is built in. Waxpad is just a markdown editor — but the files live on disk, so your AI agent reads and writes them. Here's what that unlocks:

01

Morning prep

Your agent reads your calendar and git history, fills in today's note. Open Waxpad — day's already laid out.

02

Standup prep

A brief standup from yesterday's commits. PRs shipped, bugs hit, blockers. Copy-paste into Slack.

03

Meeting prep

All context in one floating note — candidate info, colleague notes, prep questions. Visible while you're on the call.

04

After-day cleanup

Messy day notes sorted and moved to your Obsidian daily note. Close the laptop, the day is captured.

Named after the wax tablet. Romans used it to scratch notes about debts, letters, todo lists. When done, flip the stylus, smooth the wax flat. Temporary by design.

That's what Waxpad is: a surface for thoughts that don't need to live forever.
